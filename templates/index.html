About Me

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Physics and Applied Mathematics at the University of Utah. Currently, I study critical phenomena in the collapse of black holes using Numerical Relativity.

Projects

Black Hole Simulations using the Finite Difference Method

My current research investigates the behaviour of critical black hole collapse using numerical methods. In spherical symmetry critical collapse reveals a suprising degree of structure, and appears to contradict the principle of cosmic censorship. I am studying such collapse in axisymmetry to help determine how such critical phenomena changes in higher dimensional systems.
